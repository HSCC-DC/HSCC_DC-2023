Who is Gregory Williams of BDPA DC Chapter?

Hello, my name is Gregory Williams and I am on the BDPA DC chapter and I go to McKinley Technology High School and aspire to have a master-class of knowledge in computer science. On a normal day, I go home after school, do my online Udemy course, some homework, then the day is over. But on Saturdays, I go to Lorton, Virginia to participate in the weekly BDPA classes held there. In those classes we: problem solve, program, have fun, and solve bugs in those programs. After class, I will do some of my course on Udemy, homework, then it’s free time where I usually watch motivational YouTubers like Brandon Carter or JWaller. I aspire to improve my everyday hobbies to better achieve my goal of becoming a man that is influential and reliable, who’s kids will scream “Daddy’s Home” once they notice his car in the driveway. This is my goal because I believe it will give me the most satisfaction to understand what I’m surrounded by, make sure I am not someone who is easily forgotten, and have tenants for myself to be a better person, set by the creator himself. My responsibility on the HSCC team is the front-end as it is what I know best and have been focusing on for some time now, but I do endeavor to learning back-end code as it will improve my competence of computer science as a whole – especially with fully grasping the OSI model. I feel great being on the BDPA DC chapter, and I feel HSCC gives me a time limit for my goals, making me more urgent and excited to compete as I learn more and more as the years pass.
